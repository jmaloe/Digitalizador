Learning activity 2

This activity presents and extends knowledge of quantifiers, a lot, plenty of, too much, not enough, etc. through the topic of modern lifestyles. The grammar is presented through the topic of people’s work-life balance and how they feel about it.

Open the file Slow down, you move too fast from Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina. As self-study tasks answer exercises 1a, 1b and 1c.

Then, revise the Quantifiers Part 2 file, from the same author which contains information about large quantities, small quantities, zero quantity. This resource will help you to understand the use of quantifiers in English as well as the rules accompanying them.

To complete this activity write a paragraph that describes how satisfied you are with your work-life balance. It must include the quantifiers revised in the “quantifiers part 2 file”.

Check for grammar, spelling and punctuation before sending it. Do your activity in an online text. This will help you to reinforce the use of quantifiers.
