Learning activity 2

What are the best things in your town? Think about the good and bad things your town has to offer to tourist or visitor.

Using the following questions, make a brochure of your town:

  • What’s the most interesting thing to do?
  • What’s the most beautiful park?
  • Where’s the best place to take a photo of the town?
  • Where’s the most interesting local festival?
  • What’s the most typical thing to eat and drink?
  • What’s the oldest building?
  • Where’s the best shopping area?

This activity will help you reinforce the use of superlatives.

Check for grammar, punctuation and spelling mistakes before you send your brochure.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on  “Guardar cambios”.
