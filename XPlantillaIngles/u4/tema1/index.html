Content
Content

“If it is old, it is better” is a common saying of people who was born and lived some decades ago. Life has changed. We live now in larger houses, but we have smaller families. There are faster cars, but we go slower in the traffic. We work harder and we have less free time.

In this thematic unit, you will compare your lifestyle in the present with the way you lived in the past. You will also be exposed to comparative forms by reading texts and studying grammar supports. Vocabulary and expressions included in the materials are useful to express time, comparisons and thoughts.

At the end of this thematic unit, you are expected to share facts and opinions about modern life, in comparison with life before the XXI century.
