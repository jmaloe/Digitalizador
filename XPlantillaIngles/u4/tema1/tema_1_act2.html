Learning activity 2

People say living in big cities is more stressful than living in small towns. How different is your hometown from big cities?.

The following file Describing where you live from Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina contains several activities that can guide you to write a descriptive text about the place you live in.

As self-study tasks, read the text The place where I live from the same author and work on exercises a, b & c.

Finally, write a description of the place where you live. Write five paragraphs that correspond to the five questions provided in exercise a. Try to compare life in your town with life in the big cities.

Check your description for mistakes: grammar, punctuation and spelling; and attach a photo if you can. Do your activity in an online text.

This activity will help you to practice comparatives while you describe everyday life in different places.
