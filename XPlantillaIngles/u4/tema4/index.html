Content
Content

Perhaps the most demanding part of the day is the morning. In this modern lifestyle, people tend to sleep less and rush more. What time do you wake up every morning? Do you have enough time to get ready for the day?

This unit provides a gentle introduction to phrasal verbs through the topic of typical mornings in our day. The materials in the unit a range of examples about meaning and word order of phrasal verbs.

The aim of the unit is to set up the basis for phrasal verbs usage and provide you with enough practice.
