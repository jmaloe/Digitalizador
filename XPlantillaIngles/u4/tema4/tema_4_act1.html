Learning activity 1

Are you somebody who can’t wake up in the morning? How do you feel when you first wake up? What’s the first thing you turn on after you wake up?.

These and other questions are part of the file Waking up is hard to do, from Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina. Read and work on the Vocabulary exercises in this file. As you finish with exercises a & b, Open the file Vocabulary Bank Phrasal Verbs from the same author and check your answers.

Then, revise the grammar Word Order of Phrasal Verbs, which explains the rules of separable and inseparable phrasal verbs. This will help you to use phrasal verbs correctly.

The Vocabulary and Grammar self-study tasks will give you practice on word order of phrasal verbs and make you remember their meaning.

To complete this activity, record your personal answers to the seven questions included in exercise 1a of the file “Walking up is hard to do”.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on  “Guardar cambios”.
