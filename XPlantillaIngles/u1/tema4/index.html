The weekend
Working time

6 hours

Learning result(s)

Content
Content

Many of the best moments in life take place during the weekends. It is on Saturday and Sunday when most families spend time together. Almost everyone gets excited when the weekend begins, and a lot of people make plans for it. However, the weekend may also be tiring for others who deal with household chores on these days. Does the weekend have a good side and a bad side?.

The purpose of this thematic unit is to revise the Present Simple, as well as to introduce simple discursive composition using connectors. The text and activity included will provide you with grammar support and vocabulary necessary to give your opinion by writing.
