Learning activity 1

In this final activity the focus is on writing a short article giving your opinion about the weekend.

To begin with, divide a sheet of paper in two columns. Think about the good and the bad things about the weekend. Write positive ideas on one column and negative ideas in the other one.

Then, read the text Giving your opinion. Next, go through the article and instructions in exercises a and b.

Finally, follow the guidelines provided in the text “Giving your opinion” and write a three paragraph composition.

Paragraph 1: The good side

Paragraph 2: The bad side

Final paragraph

Remember the following:

  • the way Firstly, secondly are used to introduce different points.

  • the use of However to begin a paragraph which contrast with the previous one

  • the use of in general to sum up

Send your composition in an online-text.
