Content
Content

One of the greatest pleasures in life is food. Eating out or cooking at home is seen now as part of living with style. Although our eating habits and tastes may be different in each part of the world, these days people care more about eating healthy diets and avoiding fats and carbs. For all this, it is said that food conventions and dishes define us as individuals and nations.

The purpose of this thematic unit is to revise the Present Simple and the Present Continuous, as well as to distinguish between action and non-action verbs. The texts and activities included will provide you with grammar support and vocabulary necessary to describe things that are always true of happen regularly, actions in progress at the time of speaking and actions, states or feelings. The content of the unit is accompanied by a range of vocabulary related to food and restaurants. Reading, listening and writing skills will be developed as you move through the self-study tasks and exercises in this thematic unit.
