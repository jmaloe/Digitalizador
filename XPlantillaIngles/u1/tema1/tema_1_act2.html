Learning activity 2

In today’s world of e-mail communication, being able to write in English is an important skill for students of English as a foreign language.

In this activity you will consolidate the language you have learnt in the previous activity by writing an informal e-mail about yourself. To do so, open the file Describing Yourself by Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina,Read the e-mail and correct the mistakes in it. Then answer the questions provided.

To complete this activity, write a similar e-mail about you and post it in the forum: Knowing you.

Write four paragraphs.

  • Paragraph 1: name, nationality, age, family, work/study
  • Paragraph 2: physical appearance
  • Paragraph 3: personality
  • Paragraph 4: hobbies and interests

Check your writing for mistakes: grammar, punctuation and spelling.

To participate, click on “Añadir un nuevo tema de discusión”. In the pop- out that appears indicate the subject in “Asunto”. Write your comment in the box “Mensaje”; to send it, click on “Enviar al foro”.
