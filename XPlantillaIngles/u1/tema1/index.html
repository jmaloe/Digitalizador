Knowing you
Working time
Working time

6 hours

Learning result(s)
Learning result(s)

You are competent when:

  • You communicate accurately by using appropriate grammar forms.
  • You use the rhetorical forms and conventions of written discourse.
  • You develop and use a battery of listening strategies for overall comprehension.
  • You listen to specific information and identify specific information in spoken discourse.
Content
Content

Physical appearance, personality, hobbies and interests are some of the things that describe you. It is what we do every day and who we relate with what defines our personality. To express this kind of information in English, Simple Present tense is mainly used. Personal information, likes, dislikes and interests are some of the topics that will be practiced in this unit.

The aim of this unit is to revise the form and use of Simple Present tense to express things you do every day, week, year or which are always true. The activities included will provide you with grammar support and vocabulary useful to describe yourself, as well as practice on reading, listening and writing skills.
