Learning activity 2

This second activity links to the topic in activity 1. The writing practice consolidates the use of the Present Continuous and revises Prepositions of time and place as you describe what people are doing in a photo.

Choose one of your favorite photos and write a short description about it.

Remember that when you are describing a picture it is important to use the right preposition to say where things are. The following Prepositions resource displays a range of use for in, on, at.

Now write three paragraphs.

  • Paragraph 1: What’s your favorite photo? and, Why is it important to you?
  • Paragraph 2: Who is in the photo? What is happening?
  • Paragraph 3: Tell more about who or what is in the photo.

Check your writing for mistakes: grammar, punctuation and spelling. Send your description in word document. Attach a copy of the photo if you can.
