Content
Content

It is common to remember events and habits from the past when we meet people that shared those moments with us. Friends are undoubtedly witness of our life moments and with them we can recall past experiences that were remarkable in our life.

Friends and friendship provides the main topic for this thematic unit which revises and consolidates Used to- to talk about habitual actions in the past, and states and actions which are no longer true. The vocabulary focus of the thematic unit is words and phrases related to friendship. The main context is the true stories of two people who get in contact through a website to try get back in touch with old friend for their past.

With the audio material included you will develop your listening for detailed understanding and specific information, and you will be ready to record your own audio material.

In this part of the unit, you will learn how to understand, read, write, listen and speak about museums. You will learn how to respond to the rubric of a writing task and increase the awareness of text organization and descriptive styles. This theme will give you the opportunity to develop your skill to identify paragraph topics and topic sentences by reading a short article about a museum visit. You will also have the chance to use adjectives, nouns and verbs associated with a museum visit, using simple past tense to describe that visit to a museum. This theme will also give you the opportunity to develop the ability to predict and check the content of a text .
