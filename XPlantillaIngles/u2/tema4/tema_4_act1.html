Learning activity 1

Friends Reunited is the name of a popular website which helps people in Great Britain to find old friends and let them read what people, they have lost touch with, are doing now.

To carry out this activity, read the text Friends Reunited? from Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig that describes Carol´s experience in past.

Then, listen to the audio material, Carol , from the same authors mentioned, and fill in the gaps with words that are needed in the text read.

Then, listen again Carol´s audio material and in an online text answer the following questions:

  1. Why was Carol surprised at Robert´s choice of job?
  2. What happened when she got to the restaurant?
  3. What do Carol and Robert look like now?
  4. What did Carol realized as soon as she saw Robert?
  5. How had Robert changed?

After that, listen to the audio material, “Alex” , from the same authors mentioned, and fill in the gaps. Finally, Listen again and answer these questions:

  1. Did he recognize any of the people?
  2. How did he feel?
  3. What did they talk to him about?
  4. What did he remember when he saw the photos?
  5. Who is Anna? What does he think of her now?

To complete this activity send the answers to the 10 questions in online-text.

This will help you to improve your listening skill.
