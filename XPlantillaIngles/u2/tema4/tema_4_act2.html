Learning activity 2

Now you have read and listened about Carol´s and Alex´s audio material from Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig, talking about their experiences in the past, is your turn to recall what you live in the past by recording your own audio material.

You have to record yourself talking about something you lived in the past, with your friends, family, etc.

Take some ideas of the reading to facilitate your recording. Make sure your text has correct grammar and spelling.

This activity will help you to use past tenses to bring to mind situations and to develop your oral skill.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on  “Guardar cambios”.
