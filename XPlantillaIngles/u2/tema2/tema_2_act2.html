Learning activity 2

Everybody has had an anecdote at school or at playing a sport. There must be funny or interesting experiences in your life that you can share with others.

In a Word document, write an anecdote using the following guidelines:

Choose one of the topics below and plan what you are going to talk about.

  • A time you cheated (in an exam or in a sport/game)
    • What were you doing?
    • Where? When?
    • Why did you cheat?
    • What happened?
  • A time you had an accident or got a sports injury
    • What were you doing? How did the accident happen?
    • What part of your body did your hurt?
    • What happened next? How long did it take your to recover?
  • A really exciting sports event you saw
    • Where and when was it?
    • Who was playing?
    • What happened?
    • Why was it so exciting?

Use your dictionary for words you need to write your anecdote. Make sure your text has correct grammar and spelling. This activity will help you to use past tenses and to develop your writing skill.
