Reliving the past
Working time
Working time

6 hours

Learning result(s)
Learning result(s)

You are competent when:

  • You are able to look up for specific words in a dictionary.
  • You are able to narrate past events and understand texts which tell you about past events.
Content
Content

One way to revive the past is to look back at important sport events that have marked world history. It is through sports that people have found their way to improve their own capacities. A remembrance of this moment it is always part of our daily life.

In this thematic unit, you will learn how to find the meaning of new words through the text and with the use of a dictionary. You will also learn how to identify past tenses: past simple, past continuous and past perfect and use them to describe historical moments. By doing the activities you will be able to recognize correct past forms and be familiar with vocabulary about sports.
