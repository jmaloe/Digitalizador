In the course of history there have been peculiar moments that have caught people’s attention in sports, such as famous cheating moments.

After reading the text, review the following RC past tenses. It will help you to understand grammatical differences among past tenses.

As a final moment of this activity, read and complete the past tense practice, that will help you to reinforce your ability to read, write and talk about the past.
