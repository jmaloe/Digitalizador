Learning result(s)
Learning result(s)

You are competent when you:

  • Are able to use the simple past grammar structure properly to talk about past events.
  • Can write a summary taking main ideas from a text and give your opinion about it.
  • Express your ideas in oral form clearly and precisely in real situations.
  • Are able to answer questions while listening to a conversation.
Content
Content

In this part of the course, you will also develop the reading skill as you read for specific information.  Production of grammatical forms learnt by exposure to the texts will be part of this theme as well.

This theme will provide you with a range of words necessary to express past events and describe people’s life in past.
