Learning activity 1

When talking about famous people, it is important to know what events made them be famous. Before starting this topic read the text Kevin Costner and History´s hall of fame, which let you know what this topic is about.

Now that you have read about famous people’s life and recognized forms to express past events, write in a Word file your own biography and one more of a member of your family.  It should contain information about date and place of birth, important events, personal life, famous works and beliefs. It will be marked as homework #1.

This learning activity will make you practice and reinforce the use of simple past to talk about past events.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on  “Guardar cambios”.
