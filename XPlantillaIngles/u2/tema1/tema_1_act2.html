Learning activity 2

Talking and writing about the past is an everyday activity. Most the time we make reference to past events and good memories from our lives. We also look back to history and people who died years ago, but their remarkable lives have a special place in our present.

Do some research in the internet about the following famous people. Try to look for texts in English that describe their lives and their most outstanding contributions to our modern world.

  • Albert Einstein
  • William Shakespeare
  • Ludwig Van Beethoven,
  • Jean Piaget
  • Edwin Hubble
  • Hellen Keller
  • Marilyn Monroe

After this activity, you have to participate in the foro: Knowing Famous People, give your opinion about who has contributed the most to our world and why. Read other´s opinions and give a comment about that.

This learning activity will help you reinforce the use of simple past and talk about achievements in people´s life. This will be marked as homework #2.

To participate, click on “Añadir un nuevo tema de discusión”. In the pop- out that appears indicate the subject in “Asunto”. Write your comment in the box “Mensaje”; to send it, click on “Enviar al foro”.
