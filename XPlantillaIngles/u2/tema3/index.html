Homes of the past
Working time
Working time

6 hours

Learning result(s)
Learning result(s)

You are competent when:

  • You are able to narrate past events and understand a text which gives you information about homes and cities.
  • You are able to look up for specific words in a dictionary.
  • You are able to share and give information concerning your own habits and others in the past.
Content
Content

Home sweet home is always there deep inside the heart of every living individual. Having oneâ€™s home is an old dream, as old as this world is. Talk about home is always in your heart and you know the contents very well as well.

In most of the cases people change from house to house, however, most of the time they remember how were their houses when they were younger, so some talk about that topic is as simple as writing presentation of them.

In this thematic unit, you will learn how to find the meaning of new words using a dictionary. You will also learn how to identify past tenses: past simple and the function of used to form. In addition, you will learn through a description text about how to express ideas in past tense. By doing the activities you will be able to recognize correct past forms and be familiar with vocabulary about places and homes.
