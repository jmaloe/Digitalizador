Learning activity 1

When people go back to the past, and bring to mind the way they used to live, they have to use different forms of past.

In this activity you have to read the text Virginia the Old Dominion. After that, revise the information about used to and check it to facilitate the grammar structure and the function of USED TO form.

Then, write a descriptive text taking ideas from the reading, using the past form (used to). Use these questions as a guideline in order to write down your text. The text must be of 200-250 words.

  1. How did people use to live in Virginia?
  2. How did homes used to be?
  3. How was the economical and societal life?

Afterward, include a glossary of 10 words that will help you to understand and recount your text.

To complete this activity send the text and the glossary in a Word document.

Make sure your text has correct grammar and spelling. This activity will help you to support your ability to read, to increase your vocabulary and to write about the past.
