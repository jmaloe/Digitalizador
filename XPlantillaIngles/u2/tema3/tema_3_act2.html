Learning activity 2

Families used to make lots of things. Long time ago, they used to live in a different way. Cities, houses, streets, were different from those of our modern world. Undoubtedly, by taking a look at the surrounding world from the past, we can also evoke personal memories.

At this moment, it is time to share your opinions in the forum Back in Time forum in which you will recall about situation that you used to live. Use these guidelines to write your opinion.

  • What did you family use to hold?
  • Which room did you used to play?
  • What place in your town did you use to visit?

Make sure your forum message has correct grammar and spelling. This activity will help you to use past tenses, to read and interact with your partners.

To participate, click on “Añadir un nuevo tema de discusión”. In the pop- out that appears indicate the subject in “Asunto”. Write your comment in the box “Mensaje”; to send it, click on “Enviar al foro”.
