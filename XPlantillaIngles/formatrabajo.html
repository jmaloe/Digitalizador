Ways of working

This academic unit will be evaluated with the learning evidences as follows:

Online text and word documents

It is a short, clear and understandable text in which students present the result of the activity required. It must be of the length required by the instructions given in the activity. The text must be in an academic writing style with appropriate grammar, vocabulary and punctuation. An online text can take the form of a critical reflective comment which provides with answers, ideas and personal perspectives to specific questions. It can also consist of the elaboration of answers out from the text or audio material. The type of online text will be specified in the corresponding thematic unit.

Forums

It is a space for collaborative work where the student shares information with members of his/her class. It is expected to be a tool for the enrichment and exchange of well-documented participations about the topic being addressed. The information provided by the student in forum participations must reflect beliefs, ideas and points of view. Responses to peers’ contributions are required. At least three participations and three responses to peer’s contributions in forums are mandatory and will be considered for evaluation. Brief and vague participations, as well as repeating or paraphrasing other’s contributions will not be taken into account for evaluation.

Final exam

The student should demonstrate knowledge and understanding of grammar, vocabulary and other features of the language revised in the course by choosing one correct answer. The final exam will be administered at the end of the whole didactic units.
