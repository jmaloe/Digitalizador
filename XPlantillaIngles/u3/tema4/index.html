Changing your life
Working time
Working time

5 hours

Learning result(s)
Learning result(s)

You are competent when:

  • You are able to understand and use in an appropriate way the Present Perfect Continuous tense in order to express unfinished actions.
Content
Content

If you think about your life and the things you have been doing for a long time, you may find yourself having a lot of routines. How long have you been trying to become proficient in a language? How long have you been working at the same place? Changing your life requires decisiveness and courage, and not everybody is willing to do it.

In this thematic unit, we will study how to express unfinished actions by using Present Perfect Continuous forms. Listening and speaking skills will be also practiced as you moved through the activities and materials in this unit.

The materials included have been chosen for the sole purpose of giving you the necessary exposure to the language to develop communicative competence.
