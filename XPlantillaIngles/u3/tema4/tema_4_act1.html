Learning activity 1

In this activity you have to read and listen to the story of a woman who decided to change her lifestyle by taking a year off. As you work on the self-study tasks pay attention to the grammatical forms addressed.

Open the file Changing your life from the authors Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig and answer the exercises 1a, 1b and 1c. Listen to the audio material click on Karen’s interview from the same author.

As self-study tasks work on exercises 2a and 2b which present grammar forms and use of Present Perfect Continuous tense. Listen to the audio material click on Karen’s questions from the same author.

Then, review the following Present Perfect Continuous. It will help you to understand grammatical considerations of this tense.

As a final moment of this activity, take the Karen’s role and write a tape script of your own interview adding your answers to the same questions. That will help you to reinforce your ability to read, write and talk about the past.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on  “Guardar cambios”.
