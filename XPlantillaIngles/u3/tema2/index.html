Content
Content

“Home sweet home” is a cliché everybody likes to hear, but is it really true? House problems are basically those of shores and responsibilities. If a member of the family does not commit to do what he/she is in charge or expected to do, conflicts may occur. Cleaning the floor, hanging the towels, making the bed are simple things we are not always willing to do.

This thematic unit continues to work on the Present Perfect, as it is used with yet, just and already. The context is problems in the house, especially with teenagers, and the vocabulary focus is on verb phrases.

The materials and activities aim at providing you with enough exposure to the language necessary to communicate and discuss family topics.
