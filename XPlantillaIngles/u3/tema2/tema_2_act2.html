Learning activity 2

These days, it is hardly possible to find a person who does not rush during the morning, and it may be even more difficult to keep everything at the right place at home or work. Our busy agendas can make us forget doing simple things as walking the dog or turning off our computer before leaving home.

Look at the picture in Has he done it yet? file from Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig. Now, read the list of things Max always do every morning. Has he already done them? Write sentences using the following information:

  • make the bed
  • wash up his coffee cups
  • tidy his desk
  • pick up towels
  • take the dog for a walk
  • turn off the computer
  • put his clothes in the closet
  • have a shower
  • have breakfast

In an online-text, send your 8 sentences about Max using Present Perfect + just, already or yet. Check for grammar and spelling mistakes before you send them.

The learning activity will help you reinforce the use Present Perfect as you describe past events and facts that continue in the present, talking about every day issues.
