Learning activity 2

The way we use and spend our money can say a lot about our priorities and lifestyle. In today’s world we are exposed to advertising and shopping; with technology being part of our lives we can even sell and buy without going out of home.

The following file will guide you to a range of different self-study tasks that aim at preparing you for the understanding of Present Perfect tense.

Open the file Money issues from Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig, and complete the dialogue in exercise 2a. Make sure you follow the instructions to answer this exercise. Once you have completed the dialog, listen to the audio material Shelley and Ben form the same author and check your answers. As a self- study task, pay attention to exercise c. In this exercise you have to go over the dialogue and find examples of Present Perfect in it.

To continue with the activity, study the following Present Perfect and Past Simple.

Complete the activity by writing your answers to five of the questions from the Money Questionnaires in “Money issues” file. Make sure your write 5 long-explaining answers to the questions. Send your five answers in online-text.

To add your comment, click on “Agregar entrega”. Write your answer in the box that will appear. When you finish, click on "Guardar cambios". In case you want exit the editor without saving the latest changes, click on "Cancelar."
