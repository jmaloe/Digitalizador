Learning activity 1

Have you ever thought what your life would be without money? What would be different if we did not have to work or pay for services?.

The following file guides you through the reading of the text My Life without Money from Clive Oxenden and Christina Latham-Koenig. Before you read the text, do exercises 4a and 4b as self-study tasks. Then, read the article and answer the 4c exercise, the 8 questions about a woman who lives without money. After it, match the phrasal verbs from the reading with their definition in exercise 4d.

To complete this activity, revise the file Vocabulary bank and answer the Verbs, Prepositions and Nouns sections exercises.

In a word document send:

  • your answers to the 8 questions
  • the 6 phrasal verbs with their definitions
  • A paragraph explaining how you spent your money every month

Check for spelling mistakes before you submit the activity.

The self-study tasks and activity results will help you to improve your reading skill while acquiring new vocabulary.
