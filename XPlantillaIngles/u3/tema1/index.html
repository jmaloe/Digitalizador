Learning result(s)

You are competent when:

  • You are able to read short simple texts and look for specific information.
  • You are able to understand phrases and vocabulary on common areas, such as money issues.
  • You are able to communicate in oral and written form personal matters in present and past
Content
Content

Many of our current problems and concerns are related to money issues. Money can be used to get things we need and want, but it also may be the reason of disagreement. Would our life will be different or easier without money?.

In this thematic unit you will be introduced to vocabulary related to money and will read about this topic. The unit also aims at explaining Present Perfect tense form and usage in contrast to Simple Past tense as you communicate personal ideas.

The materials and activities included have the sole purpose of providing you with enough exposure to the language as you practice your reading skill. Grammar and vocabulary in this thematic unit will help you to develop your communicative competence.
