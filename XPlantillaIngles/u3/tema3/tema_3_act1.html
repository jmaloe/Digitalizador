Learning activity 1

We all have been shaped by experiences along our lives either in a positive or negative way. Many of our behaviors, fears and phobias are the result of past experiences.

Look at the text Famous Fears and Phobias, from Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina. It describes how famous people have phobias like the rest of us. As self-study tasks do exercises 1a, 1b, 1c and 1d. After that, review the following Present Perfect + for and since. It will help you understand grammatical form and use of Present Perfect tense.
