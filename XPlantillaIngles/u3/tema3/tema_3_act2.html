Learning activity 2

Most of the people we admire have interesting stories about their life before being famous. Some of them still have habits of fears, just as people like you, in spite of their richness and fame.

Do some research in the internet about a famous person you admire. You may choose an actor, singer, writer, etc. Try to look for texts in English that describe their lives and share some of his/her interesting features of their personality and successful career.

After this activity, you have to participate in the forum: Famous People Like You; share the information with your partners. Read other´s opinions and give a comment about that.

This learning activity will help you reinforce the use of Present Perfect as you describe past events and facts that continue in the present.

To participate, click on “Añadir un nuevo tema de discusión”. In the pop- out that appears indicate the subject in “Asunto”. Write your comment in the box “Mensaje”; to send it, click on “Enviar al foro”.
