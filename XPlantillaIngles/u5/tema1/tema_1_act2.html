Learning activity 2

Everybody enjoys going on holidays. No matter where we go, we all get very excited about traveling and visiting new places.

Revise the file Holidays from Oxeden, Clive, & Latham-Koenig Christina; which includes vocabulary to express ideas related to holiday activities and the weather. As a self-study task, revise and complete the exercises in this file.

Finish this activity by reading the article New York from Santos, Thomas. It describes parks, famous buildings, neighborhoods, art and entertainment of this city.

After that, write an e-mail to a friend describing your plans and arrangements for your holiday in this city. Your e-mail must show evidence of grammar and vocabulary revised in this thematic unit. Check for grammar and spelling before you send your work.

This activity will help you to reinforce the use of going to and present continuous for future arrangements. Submit your work in a Word file.

This file must be saved with a name alluding to the subject. To send it, click on “Agregar entrega”. Then, drag and drop the file to start loading. Once complete it, click on “Guardar cambios”.
