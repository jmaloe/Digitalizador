Content
Content

Planning our future is in what we spend most of our time in the present. Most people write in their agendas or calendar everything they have to do. We tend to schedule every single event from birthdays and meetings, to holidays.

In this unit, you will revise going to, to talk about plans and predictions, and also learn a new use of the present continuous to talk about fixed plans and arrangements. The activities will provide you with grammar supports and vocabulary useful to express plans in the future. The activities included will give you practice on writing letters and e-mails with appropriate grammar, spelling and punctuation.
