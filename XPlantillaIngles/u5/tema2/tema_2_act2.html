Knowing you

What will happen to our world in the next fifty years? How will life be different?

In a recent report, the following prediction were made about British family life:

  • In the year 2020, the divorce will be over 50%.
  • In the year 2020, one in three people will be living alone.
  • In the year 2020, children will be using computers at the age of three.

Look for other predictions in scientific magazines of the Web about years 2020 and 2050 and share them with students in the Forum About the future. You may include the following topics in your research: family, relationships, jobs, weather, pollution, technology, science, education, etc. Begin your predictions with:

  • By the year 2050, …
  • In fifty years, …
  • Sometime soon, …
  • In the year…

Do not forget to use appropriate grammar, punctuation and capitalization in the messages you post, as well as to read and answer other students’ messages by expressing agreement or disagreement. Remember it is very important to answer other partners to facilitate the interaction with them.

This activity will help you to reinforce the use of future tenses and the language necessary for interaction. You must not upload word files in the forum of this activity.
