Content
Content

What will you be doing this time next year? This might be an easy question for those who plan ahead and dream of the future, but it may be a difficult question for others. Lennon´s famous quote says, “Life is what happens while you are busy making other plans”. How many times have you found yourself thinking how your life will be in the future?

In this unit, you will revise Future Continuous, and also learn a new use of the present continuous to talk about fixed plans and arrangements. The activities will provide you with grammar supports and vocabulary useful to express plans in the future. The activities included will give you practice on writing formal texts.
